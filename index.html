Website Badan Amil Zakat Nasional Surakarta

The National Amil Zakat Agency (BAZNAS) is the official and only body established by the government based on the Decree of the President of the Republic of Indonesia No. 8 of 2001 which has the task and function of collecting and distributing zakat, infaq, and alms (ZIS) at the national level. The enactment of Law Number 23 of 2011 concerning Zakat Management further strengthens the role of BAZNAS as an institution authorized to manage zakat nationally. In the law, BAZNAS is declared as a non-structural government institution that is independent and responsible to the President through the Minister of Religion.
